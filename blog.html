Top Resort in Zuluk

Zuluk or DZuluk is a small area located at an altitude of 10,000 meters (3,000 m) in the rugged Himalayas lowlands of East Sikkim in the Indian state of Sikkim. This area is by far the most emerging and low-income area in East Sikkim. Today it is rapidly emerging as a new tourist destination. It finds its value in tourism due to its magnificent view of the eastern Himalayan mountains. Zuluk itself does not offer glimpses of the peaks but only 14 km away from Thambi View Point offers a panoramic view of the entire Kanchenjungha range.
