ATTRACTIONS AROUND Chatakpur:

Tiger Hill - Tiger Hill offers beautiful panoramas of the Greater Himalayas. Kanchenjunga, the third highest peak in the world, is illuminated at sunset and at sunrise in colorful displays. Mt Everest and a few other mountain peaks of the 8000 + m club can easily be seen on this mountain. You can even see the city of Kurseong on a sunny day, the destination itself offers not only beautiful views during sunrise, a place where you can immerse yourself in the beauty of the Himalayas at any time during the day.
Senchal Lake - Senchal Lake is a beautiful water feature located in the Senchal Wildlife Sanctuary. The lake offers amazing views of the hilly environment. The lake water is clean and potable, which is why it is supplied throughout Darjeeling. Surrounded by dense forests of oak, pine and birch, this is a picture of a place where you can discover nature in a very vivid way. Colorful flowers like rhododendrons and orchids on the lake shore enhance the beauty.
Senchal Wildlife Sanctury - The dense canopy of the Senchal Wildlife Sanctuary is home to a wide variety of wildlife and birds. In fact you may be surprised to see birds such as the Himalayan Bulbul, Blue magpie, warbler and Verdant Flycatcher often appear. As for the wildlife, if you are lucky enough, you can watch the Great Himalayan Black Bear, the mountain leopard and the barking deer.
Runglee Rungliot Tea Estate - Among the most popular Tea sites in the Darjeeling region, Runglee Rungliot is one of them. It is actually one of the oldest Duncans tea varieties. One can visit the estate factories and watch tea processing but not winter as it is usually closed in winter. A beautiful view from the tea garden will surely delight the person. You can explore the area by walking through the tea gardens and interacting with local people.

How to reach Chatakpur:

Chatakpur is around 16 kilometres from Jorebungalow that lies on Siliguri-Darjeeling Road and 7 kilometres from Sonada. You need to go through the Hill Road and enter the jungle trail, in order to reach Chatakpur.
Air Route The nearest airport is Bagdogra. It is 80 km away and connects with Delhi, Guwahati and Kolkata.
Rail Route Ghoom is the nearest station connected by Indian Heritage Toy Train with New Jalpaiguri and Siliguri Junction station which are nearest broad gauge railway station and is 87 km away.
Road NH 55 passes along the Western side of the Sanctuary . The sanctuary is 9 km away from Darjeeling. Chatakpur Eco village is 17 km from Ghoom, Jorebunglow and 7 km from Sonada.
